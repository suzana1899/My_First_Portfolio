Understanding HTML Headings

Headings are important in structuring your content. They range from level 1 to level 6, with <h1> being the most important and <h6> the least. They help search engines and users navigate your content easily.

Importance of Paragraphs

Paragraphs are used to organize text into readable blocks. It helps in improving the readability of the webpage. Each paragraph tag <p> can contain text, links, or even images, making it highly versatile.

In addition to headings and paragraphs, images can also be added to a webpage. Images can be made clickable, allowing them to link to another website, providing additional resources or information to the user.
